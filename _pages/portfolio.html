Video of my robot for the Royal Academy Event


This video shows some of the work from my PhD, including a robot I built and tested with sensors for navigation and inspection of a bridge in Leeds. The aim of this work is to be able to perform autonomous inspection of the bridge bearings, including monitoring the bearings for changes in geometry and crack detection.



Video of The Infrastructure Challenge Competition


This video shows some of the proof-of-concept work for using a drone for monitoring, tracking and directing construction vehicles to improve safety in an open mine or construction site.


National Prototyping Workshop

This video shows the ESPRC National Prototyping workshop that I participated in in 2016.


University of Leeds - 3D printing of Asphalt

This video shows some of the work from the self-repairing cities project for drones to repair potholes. I am currently working on the computer vision system for detecting cracks in concrete and asphalt surfaces.


University of Leeds - Search and Rescue

I helped team members from the University of Leeds set up computer vision and robotics systems to go onboard our drones.
